Top 10 Best and Worst Cars of 2021



About my Website

In the following website, I have made a list that ranks the top 5 best and worst cars of 2021, and why they are so good or so bad. The purpose of this list is to inform and educate those who are interested in cars and why some are better than others. In the website I list the price, the quality and the country in which the car was made. I hope you like my website and learn something new. Enjoy!




1. The worst cars in the world : The Volkswagen Atlas 2021

this year is that it received a 1 out of 5 reliability rating because

of leaky gas tanks or airbag issues.

Although the Volkswagen company is originally German, the Atlas is

manufactured in Tennessee, and is priced at about $55,000 cad.

In 1925, the Jeep company went American when Chrysler bought AMC,

4. The worst cars in the world : The GMC Canyon 2021

The GMC Canyon 2021 has a few terrible ratings from owner

5. The worst cars in the world : Ford EcoSport 2021

The Ford EcoSport 2021 has a few problems such as a

1. The Best cars in the world : The Lexus ES Hybrid 2021

The Lexus ES Hybrid 2021 is an extremely high rated

car in terms of reliability, and it's also a hybrid meaning

it is partially economically friendly.

The Lexus ES Hybrid 2021 is manufactured in Kentucky

and is priced at around $62,000 cad.


2. The Best cars in the world : 2021 Genesis G80

The 2021 Genesis G80 has incredible suspension and driver assist

to make for an overall smooth ride. It has a 100,000 mile warranty

and is meant for those who are looking for luxury.

The 2021 Genesis G80 is manufactured in South Korea, and is

priced at around $66,000 cad for the Advanced model and $76,000 cad

3. The Best cars in the world : 2021 Acura TLX

The 2021 Acura TLX is a fairly reliable car that can last 250,000

to 300,000 miles. It has a sleek outer shell and provides a smooth ride.

It is equipped with a turbocharged 4 cylinder engine for quick acceleration

and is built for luxury.

The 2021 Acura TLX is manufactored in Ohio, and is priced

at around $44,000 cad, which is a great price for such a nice car.


4. The Best cars in the world : 2021 Mercedez-Benz C-Class

The 2021 Mercedez-Benz is an all around good car, but

it especially exceeds with its premium feel, advanced technological

features and sleek, classy design.

The 2021 Mercedez-Benz C-Class is made in Bremen,

Germany, and is priced at around $50,000 cad to $60,000 cad.
